About the Fotobox

The Fotobox is the must-have on your party.
An integrated high quality reflex camera controlled by a raspberry pi takes great pictures at your event. The pictures are immediately uploaded, so that users can share them instantenously to promote your event. A thermal printer creates a receipt with a preview image and a QR code.
